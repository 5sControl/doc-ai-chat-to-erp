Summarize any content in seconds. Then quiz yourself, save key cards, translate words, hear text aloud, and get diagrams on any topic.

WHAT YOU CAN SUMMARIZE
• Paste a link (article, YouTube, etc.) or paste text
• Upload files: PDF, DOC, DOCX, RTF, TXT, ePub, video
• Share from Chrome, Safari, or other apps → open in Summify → get a summary

FEATURES

• Brief & Deep summaries — short overview or full breakdown
• Knowledge cards — AI extracts key ideas, terms, and insights; save cards to a collection and review later
• Quiz — check your understanding with auto-generated questions and explanations
• Translation — tap any word or phrase to translate (supports many languages)
• Text to speech (TTS) — listen to the summary or selected text with natural voice
• Document research — ask questions about the topic; get answers with diagrams (flowcharts, etc.) you can open full-screen and zoom
• Saved cards — build your own collection of key ideas from different docs; filter by type (thesis, term, conclusion, insight)

LEARN LANGUAGES FROM DOCS
Read in your language, translate words on tap, listen to pronunciation. Use summaries, cards, and quizzes to study faster. Ideal for students, professionals, and anyone learning a language from real content.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Open Summify, paste a URL, upload a file, or paste text.
2. Get your brief or deep summary.
3. Use the tabs: Quiz, Knowledge cards, Research (Q&A and diagrams). Tap words to translate, use the play button to hear text.

Multilingual interface: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Polish, Portuguese, Russian, Turkish, Vietnamese, Chinese. Copy and share summaries anytime.
